# Skill: Campaign Planner

> Plans multi-post social media campaigns for Kyro with content calendars, post sequences, and cross-platform strategy.

---

## Inputs

| Parameter | Required | Description | Example |
|-----------|----------|-------------|---------|
| `theme` | Yes | Campaign theme or goal | "Launch push notifications feature" |
| `duration` | Yes | Campaign length | `1-week`, `2-weeks`, `1-month`, `sprint-3-days` |
| `platforms` | No | Target platforms (defaults to all) | `["instagram", "tiktok", "linkedin"]` |
| `goal` | No | Primary KPI | `awareness`, `signups`, `engagement`, `feature-adoption` |
| `audience` | No | Primary audience segment | `cafe-owners`, `retail`, `salon-owners`, `all-smb` |
| `budget` | No | Paid promotion budget | `organic-only`, `low`, `medium`, `high` |

---

## Content Pillars Reference

All campaign content must map to one of these pillars:

| Pillar | Weight | Purpose |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **Product Power** | 30% | Show what Kyro does and why it matters |
| **Social Proof** | 25% | Customer stories, results, testimonials |
| **Education** | 25% | Teach loyalty, marketing, and local biz tips |
| **Brand Personality** | 10% | Behind the scenes, culture, humor |
| **Local Biz Love** | 10% | Celebrate and spotlight local businesses |

Campaigns should hit multiple pillars across the post sequence while staying anchored to the theme.

---

## Output Structure

### 1. Campaign Overview
- **Campaign name**: Catchy internal name
- **Theme**: Core message
- **Goal**: Primary objective and how to measure it
- **Duration**: Start to end
- **Target audience**: Who this is for
- **Key message**: The single takeaway if someone sees only one post
- **Content mix**: Pillar distribution for this specific campaign

### 2. Campaign Narrative Arc

Map the campaign to a storytelling structure:

| Phase | Posts | Purpose | Tone |
|-------|-------|---------|------|
| **Tease** | 1-2 | Create curiosity, hint at what's coming | Mysterious, intriguing |
| **Launch** | 2-3 | Reveal the main message, biggest impact content | Bold, confident |
| **Educate** | 2-4 | Go deeper, show how it works, prove value | Helpful, authoritative |
| **Prove** | 1-3 | Social proof, results, testimonials | Authentic, proud |
| **Close** | 1-2 | Final CTA, urgency or recap | Direct, warm |

### 3. Content Calendar

For each post, provide:

```
POST [number] | [Day/Date] | [Platform(s)]
═══════════════════════════════════════════
Pillar: [content pillar]
Phase: [tease/launch/educate/prove/close]
Format: [reel/carousel/single-image/story/text-post]

CAPTION BRIEF:
- Hook: [first line / scroll-stopper]
- Core message: [main point in 1-2 sentences]
- CTA: [specific call to action]

VISUAL BRIEF:
- Format: [specs]
- Style: [description]
- Key visual: [what the viewer sees]
- Colors: [primary palette for this post]

HASHTAGS: [platform-appropriate set]

POSTING TIME: [optimal time window]
BOOST: [yes/no, if yes: target audience, budget allocation]

CROSS-PLATFORM NOTES:
- [How to adapt this post for other platforms]
```

### 4. Cross-Platform Strategy

Define how content flows across platforms:

| Platform | Role in Campaign | Content Types | Posting Frequency |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Instagram | Primary showcase | Reels, carousels, stories | Daily during campaign |
| TikTok | Reach and virality | Short-form video, trends | 1-2x/day during launch |
| LinkedIn | Professional credibility | Text posts, articles | 2-3x/week |
| Twitter/X | Real-time engagement | Hot takes, threads | Daily |
| Facebook | Community reach | Shared from IG + native | 3-4x/week |

Include repurposing matrix: how one piece of content becomes 3-5 platform-specific posts.

### 5. A/B Testing Plan

For each testable element, define:

| Test | Variable A | Variable B | Metric | Duration |
|------|-----------|-----------|--------|----------|
| Hook style | Question hook | Bold statement hook | Stop rate, engagement | First week |
| CTA type | "Try it free" | "See how it works" | Click-through rate | Full campaign |
| Visual style | Dark bg + lime | Light bg + purple | Engagement rate | Full campaign |
| Post time | 8-9 AM | 12-1 PM | Reach, engagement | Full campaign |
| Format | Reel vs carousel | - | Saves, shares | First 5 posts |

Include instructions for:
- What to measure for each test
- When to call a winner
- How to apply learnings to remaining posts

### 6. Engagement Strategy

- **Response templates**: Pre-written replies for common comments
- **Community interaction**: How to engage with the audience during the campaign
- **UGC prompts**: How to encourage user-generated content
- **Story engagement**: Polls, questions, quizzes related to the campaign

### 7. Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Reach | [specific target] | Platform analytics |
| Engagement rate | [target %] | Likes + comments + shares / reach |
| Link clicks | [target number] | UTM tracking |
| Signups / conversions | [target number] | Kyro dashboard |
| Content saves | [target number] | Platform analytics |
| Brand mentions | [target number] | Social listening |

## Campaign Templates

### Feature Launch (1 week)
- Day 1: Tease (cryptic visual, "Something's coming")
- Day 2: Tease (behind-the-scenes, countdown)
- Day 3: Launch (hero reel, full announcement)
- Day 4: Educate (how it works, tutorial)
- Day 5: Prove (early adopter results or demo)
- Day 6: Educate (tips and tricks, use cases)
- Day 7: Close (recap + CTA, limited-time offer)

### Brand Awareness (2 weeks)
- Week 1: Problem-aware content (pain points local businesses face)
- Week 2: Solution-aware content (how Kyro solves each pain point)
- Mix of education, product, and social proof throughout
- End with conversion-focused posts

### Customer Spotlight (1 week)
- Day 1: Introduce the business
- Day 2: Their challenge (before Kyro)
- Day 3: The solution (implementing Kyro)
- Day 4: The results (numbers, quotes)
- Day 5: Lessons for other businesses + CTA

### Seasonal/Event (sprint, 3 days)
- Day 1: Timely hook + relevance to loyalty
- Day 2: Actionable tip + product tie-in
- Day 3: Strong CTA with urgency

## Example Execution

**Input:**
- Theme: "Launch of push notifications feature"
- Duration: `1-week`
- Platforms: `["instagram", "tiktok", "linkedin"]`
- Goal: `feature-adoption`
- Audience: `all-smb`

**Output (abbreviated):**

### Campaign Overview
- **Name**: "The Nudge" Campaign
- **Theme**: Kyro now sends push notifications that bring customers back
- **Goal**: Drive 500 existing merchants to activate push notifications within 7 days
- **Key message**: Your customers opted in. Now you can reach them for free, anytime.
- **Content mix**: Product Power 40%, Education 30%, Social Proof 20%, Brand Personality 10%

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Every post maps to a content pillar
- [ ] Campaign has a clear narrative arc (not just random posts)
- [ ] Cross-platform strategy defined (not just copy-paste)
- [ ] A/B tests are specific and measurable
- [ ] Voice is consistent throughout (Straight Up Friendly)
- [ ] CTAs vary and escalate through the campaign
- [ ] Posting times are specified and realistic
- [ ] Success metrics are defined with actual targets
- [ ] No post uses banned words or corporate language
- [ ] Campaign can be executed by a small team (realistic scope)
