## Prompt Engineering Tips for Higgsfield AI

### Getting the best results

1. **Be specific about camera movement.** Use terms like "camera slowly pushes in," "top-down view," "handheld feel," "pulls back to reveal." Vague prompts produce generic movement.

2. **Describe lighting explicitly.** "Warm morning light streaming through a window" beats "well-lit." Mention direction (side-lit, backlit, overhead) and quality (soft, harsh, diffused).

3. **Anchor your colors in objects, not abstract instructions.** Instead of "use lime green," say "a lime-green glow emanates from the card edge." Tie color to a physical element.

4. **Layer your prompt: subject, action, environment, mood.** Structure prompts as: "A [subject] [does action] in [environment]. [Mood/style descriptor]. [Camera/technical direction]."

5. **Specify what NOT to include.** Add "No text overlays. No watermarks. No faces visible." if needed. Negative prompting reduces unwanted elements.

6. **Keep people descriptions broad for consistency.** Say "a young woman" rather than describing detailed features. This avoids uncanny-valley results.

7. **Reference real cinematography styles.** Phrases like "shot on 35mm," "anamorphic lens flare," "shallow depth of field at f/1.4" guide the visual quality.

8. **Use duration to control complexity.** 3-second clips work for simple motions (number animations, logo reveals). 10-15 seconds for scenes with narrative progression.

### Compositing AI video with brand overlays

1. **Generate at the highest resolution available** (typically 1080p). Upscale with Topaz or similar if needed.
2. **Export with transparent or solid black backgrounds** for abstract shots to simplify compositing.
3. **Add brand elements in post-production** (After Effects, CapCut Pro, or Canva Video):
   - Kyro logo watermark: bottom-right, 60px from edges, 30% opacity for subtle placement or 100% for branded content.
   - Text overlays: Use the brand fonts (see social-design-specs.md) with proper safe zones.
   - Color grade the AI output to match the Kyro palette -- push blacks toward #0B051D, tint highlights toward #E6FFA9.
4. **Create a motion graphics template** (.mogrt for Premiere or preset in CapCut) with:
   - Intro bumper (logo animation, 1-2s)
   - Lower third for captions
   - End card with CTA
5. **Layer structure** (bottom to top): AI video base, color correction, vignette, text/UI overlays, logo, captions.

### Post-production workflow

1. Generate the AI clip using the prompt.
2. Review and select the best generation (run 3-4 generations per prompt).
3. Trim to exact duration needed.
4. Color grade to Kyro palette.
5. Add brand overlays and text in editing software.
6. Export per platform specs (see social-design-specs.md).
7. Add captions/subtitles if dialogue or key text is present.
